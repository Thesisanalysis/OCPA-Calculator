What it does

This PAU OCPA Predictor tool helps students estimate their Overall Cumulative Performance Average (OCPA) based on their current and expected grades. You can manually enter your cumulative credit hours and points, along with the details of your current semester courses, to get an accurate prediction of your final OCPA.

Who it's for

This predictor is specifically designed for students at institutions like **Punjab Agricultural University (PAU)** who need to calculate and project their OCPA. It's an essential tool for those who want to:

  • Monitor their academic progress and performance.
  • Set grade goals for the current semester.
  • Forecast their final OCPA for job applications or higher studies.

Benefits

Using the OCPA Predictor tool offers significant advantages:

  • **Accurate Projections:** The tool uses accurate formulas to give you a reliable estimate of your OCPA.
  • **Easy to Use:** The manual entry system is straightforward, allowing you to quickly input your data.
  • **Academic Planning:** By predicting your OCPA, you can make informed decisions about your academic goals and course load.
  • **Data Export:** You can export your results to an HTML report or a CSV file for your personal records or to share with advisors.

How to Use

  1. **Enter Student Information:** Fill in your name, admission number, and current semester.
  2. **Add Cumulative Data:** Enter your "Cumulative Credit Hours" and "Cumulative Credit Points" from your last CP sheet. For first-semester students, enter '0'.
  3. **Input Current Courses:** Add each course for your current semester, including its credit hours and expected percentage grade.
  4. **Calculate:** Click the "Calculate OCPA" button to see your results, including your current semester CPA and projected overall OCPA.
